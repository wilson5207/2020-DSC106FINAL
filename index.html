Looking at the graph above, we can clearly see a positive relationship between a team's total salary and its performance in season 2019-2020. However, this positive relationship is not strong, and there are many outliers as well. For example, Minnesota Timberwolves (MIN) has the most total salary, yet it has a horrible season in 2019-2020, winning only 30% of the games. An example that fits our positive relationship would be the Milwaukee Bucks (MIL). This team has the most winning percentage and good amount of total salary.
